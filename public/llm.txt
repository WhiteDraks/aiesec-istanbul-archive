# AIESEC İstanbul Alumni Archive

Welcome! This document provides information about the AIESEC İstanbul Alumni Archive project for AI agents, crawlers, and LLMs.

## Overview
AIESEC İstanbul Alumni Archive is a private archive and networking platform dedicated to preserving the leadership history, memory, and records of past Executive Board (EB) teams of AIESEC İstanbul (founded in 1954). It serves as a digital bridge between generations of AIESEC leaders, enabling networking, mentorship, and historical preservation.

## Key Features
1. **Executive Board (EB) Gallery & Teams:** Chronological display of leadership teams from 1954 to the present. Dynamically generated team pages displaying the LCP, LCVP, and other board members.
2. **Alumni Directory:** A searchable and filterable directory of approved alumni members. Search by name, company, school, sector, city, or AIESEC year. Includes mentorship matching.
3. **Memories & Stories:** A platform for sharing historical photos, anecdotes, and memories from past terms.
4. **Alumni Career History:** Flexible roles history management where users can input custom years in YYYY-YYYY format.
5. **Admin panel:** Controls user approvals, user roles, site configuration, and database synchronization.

## Technology Stack
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, Express Session
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Neon database / serverless), pg-session-store
- **Frontend:** HTML5, EJS templates, Vanilla CSS, Vanilla JavaScript, Cropper.js for images
- **Media Uploads:** Vercel Blob storage, AWS S3 S3-compatible backend
- **Security:** Helmet CSP (Content Security Policy), Origin & Referer CSRF checks, BcryptJS password hashing, Content Creation Rate Limiters
